About the Game

Picross Logic is a classic nonogram puzzle game rooted in logic and deduction. Each puzzle presents players with a grid and numeric clues along the rows and columns.

By interpreting these clues and determining which squares to fill or mark, players gradually reveal a hidden picture beneath the surface.

We wanted to make something that's chill but still makes your brain work - you'll pick it up quickly, but trust me, some of these puzzles will have you scratching your head.
